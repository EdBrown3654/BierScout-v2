# Brand Brief

## Identity
- Brand name: `BIERSCOUT`
- Brand promise: `Hopfen, Humor und Harte Rabatte`
- Brand personality: `Bold, unapologetic, irreverent, raw, fun – like a beer-loving punk zine`

## Visual direction
- Color direction: `Black & white primary, amber/gold (#D4A017) accent, occasional red (#FF0000) for emphasis. High contrast brutalist palette.`
- Typography direction: `Monospace or heavy grotesque for headings (system-ui, Courier, or Impact). Raw, unpolished feel. ALL CAPS for headings.`
- Shape language: `Sharp rectangles, thick borders (3-5px solid black), no rounded corners, grid-based layout with intentional asymmetry`
- Do-not-use rules: `No gradients, no shadows, no rounded corners, no stock photography, no pastel colors, no smooth animations`

## Logo guidance
- Preferred logo style: `Text-only brutalist wordmark in heavy weight`
- Symbol ideas: `Hop cone, beer mug, scout binoculars – all in stark black/white`
- Text treatment: `ALL CAPS, monospace or impact font, thick border around text`

## Image guidance
- Hero image style: `Bold typography-driven, no hero images – text IS the hero`
- Social image style: `Black background, white text, beer emoji accents`
- Background direction: `Flat white or flat black, no textures or patterns`

## Technical output
- Preferred output formats: `SVG for logos, WebP for any images`
- Preferred dimensions: `Responsive-first, mobile 375px to desktop 1440px`
- Accessibility constraints: `WCAG AA contrast minimum, keyboard navigable, screen reader compatible`
